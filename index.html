Media Working Group

What we do

The goal of the Media Working Group is to develop and improve client-side media processing and playback features on the Web. Learn more about the goals, scope, and deliverables.

Who we are

The chairs of the Media Working Group are:

  • Mounir Lamouri (Google)
  • Jer Noble (Apple)

The W3C Team Contact for the Media Working Group is François Daoust <fd@w3.org>.

Also see the list of participants.

Meetings

Kick-off face-to-face meeting planned on 19-20 September 2019 during W3C's annual Technical Plenary Advisory Committee (TPAC) event.

Get involved

This group works in public. We welcome comments and discussion from the web community, on GitHub and our mailing-list public-media-wg@w3.org (publicly archived).
Notes get taken during meetings on IRC: irc.w3.org:6665#mediawg / IRC web client

For further information, please email the Team Contact for the Media Working Group:
François Daoust <fd@w3.org>.
